Principles of using cookies

In compliance with § 55 par. 5 Act of the National Council of the Slovak Republic No. 351/2011 Coll. on Electronic Communications as amended we kindly inform you about using cookie files and draw your attention to a possibility of changing the setting of your browser in case the current setting for using cookie files is not convenient for you.

What are cookie files?

Cookie files are small text files which can be sent to browser when you visit websites and saved to your device (computer or other device with Internet access, i. e. smartphone or tablet). Cookie files are saved in the folder for files of your browser. Cookie files usually contain the name of the website they come from and the date of origin. Upon your next visit the browser loads cookie files again and sends these information back to the website which originally created cookie files. Cookie files we use do not harm your computer.

Using cookie files

By using these websites you give your consent to using cookie files in compliance with your browser setting. If you visit our websites, acceptance of cookie files is allowed in your browser, you do not change your browser setting and continue visiting our websites we consider it as accepting our conditions for using cookie files.

Why are we using cookie files?

Cookie files are used to optimally create and constantly improve the quality of our services, adjust them to your interests and needs and improve their structure and contents. We do not use data obtained by using cookie files to contact users by mail, e-mail or telephone.

How to change setting of cookie files?

Majority of browsers has been originally set to automatically accept cookie files. This setting can be changed by blocking cookie files or by a notification in case cookie files are to be sent to your device. Instructions for changing cookie files are available in the option “Help” of every browser. If you use different devices to access websites (e. g. computer, smartphone, tablet), we recommend you to adjust every browser on each of them to your preferences regarding cookie files.

Why to keep setting of cookie files?

Using cookie files and permitting cookie files in your browser is your decision. However, in case you change setting of cookie files, functionality of some of our websites can be limited and their user comfort reduced.
